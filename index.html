Careers

Join Emancro and help empower humanity through robotics using deep technical insights derived from cutting edge research.

Founding Navigation and Path Planning Engineer

You will own a significant part of the navigation stack of an autonomous hospital logistics robot.
Desired technical skills:
  • Experience developing and deploying autonomously navigating robots
  • Expert-level knowledge of SLAM
  • Expert-level knowledge of techniques for sensor fusion, including Kalman Filters
  • Expert-level knowledge of path-planning algorithms
  • Expert-level ROS user (python and C++)
Start date: As soon as possible.
Location: Berkeley, CA, in-person remote-hybrid possible

Founding Robotics Mechatronics Engineer

You will play a key role in designing mechanical parts, electronics and control systems for a hospital logistics robot.
Desired technical skills:
  • Expert-level Solidworks user
  • Extensive experience with Arduino and electronics
  • Experience designing and tuning control systems (PID)
  • Experience with ROS (python and C++)
  • 3D printing experience
Start date: As soon as possible.
Location: Berkeley, CA, In-person
